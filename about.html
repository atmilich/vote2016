About Vote2016.com

Vote2016.com is a website that allows users to compare and contrast the political beliefs of the frontrunning candidates for the 2016 presidential election. The website was created in August 2015 as the final project of a two week web design course at the Flatiron School in New York City. The creators, Alexandra Milich (Dalton Class of 2017) and Celia Heath (Dalton Class of 2018) worked on the project for 3 days, utilizing the HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills that they had learned throughout the course.
